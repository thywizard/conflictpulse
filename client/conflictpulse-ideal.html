Under The Hood

Automated Pipeline.
Zero Manual Curation.

From raw RSS feeds to scored, deduplicated, AI-summarized intelligence — fully automated.

1
RSS Fetch
Every 10 minutes, our backend fetches the latest headlines from 8 global news sources automatically.
2
AI Processing
New articles are batch-sent to Gemini 2.0 Flash, which summarizes, categorizes, scores, and extracts entities.
3
Deduplication
AI detects duplicate stories from multiple sources — only unique, conflict-related stories are saved.
4
Live Feed
Processed articles appear in the feed, sorted by recency, with filters, search, and conflict scoring ready to use.
