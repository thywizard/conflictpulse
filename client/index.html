Under The Hood

How ConflictPulse Works

Fully automated pipeline from raw RSS to curated intelligence.

1
RSS Fetch
Every 10 minutes, our backend automatically fetches the latest headlines from 8 global news sources.
2
AI Processing
New articles are sent to Gemini 2.0 Flash, which summarizes, categorizes, scores severity, and extracts entities.
3
Deduplication
AI detects duplicate stories from multiple sources — only unique, conflict-related stories are saved to the database.
4
Live Feed
Processed articles appear in the feed sorted by recency, with filters, search, and conflict scoring ready to use.
