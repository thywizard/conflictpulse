Under The Hood

How ConflictPulse Works

Fully automated pipeline from raw RSS to curated intelligence.

1
RSS Fetch
Every 10 minutes, our backend fetches the latest headlines from 8 global news sources automatically.
2
AI Processing
New articles are batch-sent to Gemini 2.0 Flash, which summarizes, categorizes, scores, and extracts entities.
3
Deduplication
AI detects duplicate stories covering the same event from multiple sources — only unique stories are saved.
4
Live Feed
Processed articles appear instantly in the feed, sorted by recency, with filters and search ready to use.
