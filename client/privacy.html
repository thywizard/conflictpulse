Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025 | Effective: April 2025

ConflictPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using ConflictPulse, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate and improve the service:

  • Usage Data: Page views, articles read, time spent on site, device type (mobile/desktop/tablet), and referrer URL. This is collected anonymously via our own analytics system.
  • Visitor ID: A randomly generated anonymous identifier stored in your browser's localStorage. This is not linked to any personal data.
  • Interaction Data: Article likes, shares, and saves are tracked anonymously to improve content ranking.
  • Local Storage: We store your liked articles, saved articles, and preferences locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

ConflictPulse uses:

  • localStorage: To remember your saved articles, liked articles, and display preferences. This stays on your device.
  • Google AdSense Cookies: Our advertising partner Google uses cookies to serve relevant ads. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use lightweight anonymous analytics to understand how people use the site.

You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies by visiting www.aboutads.info.

3. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

Google's advertising requirements can be summed up by Google's Advertising Principles. They are put in place to provide a positive experience for users. See Google's advertising policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the ConflictPulse service
  • To understand which content is most valuable to our readers
  • To detect and prevent abuse
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve the user experience

5. Information We Do NOT Collect

ConflictPulse does not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifying information (no account required)
  • Your exact location or IP address beyond general country-level data
  • Any payment information
  • Any sensitive personal data

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Typography delivery. No personal data is shared.
  • Cloudinary — Image hosting for article images.
  • MongoDB Atlas — Database hosting for article storage.
  • Render — Backend API hosting.

7. GDPR / CCPA Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data processing
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Since we do not collect personal data, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. For any concerns, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

ConflictPulse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect. All connections to ConflictPulse are encrypted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

11. Contact Us
