Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

ConflictPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate ConflictPulse:

  • Anonymous visitor ID: A randomly generated ID stored in your browser (localStorage) to track basic usage statistics. It contains no personal information.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, articles read, device type (mobile/desktop), and referring website. This is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Local preferences: Articles you like or save are stored only on your device in localStorage and never sent to our servers.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

ConflictPulse uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to show you personalized ads based on your interests.

  • Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to ConflictPulse and/or other sites on the internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out via aboutads.info.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, see: How Google uses data from sites that use our services.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — for advertising. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN.
  • ConflictPulse API — our own backend (hosted on Render.com) which serves article data. No personal data is stored on this server beyond anonymous visit counts.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not require account registration or login.
  • We do not sell any data to third parties.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or fingerprinting techniques beyond standard Google AdSense cookies.

5. Children's Privacy

ConflictPulse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to delete your data (you can clear localStorage in your browser settings)
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising (see Google Ads Settings above)

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the ConflictPulse GitHub repository at github.com/thywizard/conflictpulse.

By using ConflictPulse, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
